Diabetes Detection in Women
with DiaResFormer

A hybrid deep learning explainable framework to assess diabetes risk in women from clinical data. Every prediction comes with a full explainability report.

Key Features

Deep Learning Hybrid Model

A hybrid model combining TabResNet and FT-Transformer. Trained on three diverse datasets:

  • National Institute of Diabetes (NIDDK)
  • Hospital Frankfurt, Germany
  • Pabna Diabetes Hospital, Bangladesh

XAI Integration

Every prediction is accompanied by four complementary explainability methods:

  • Counterfactual Explanations
  • Permutation Feature Importance (PFI)
  • ALE (Accumulated Local Effects)
  • Attention Rollout

Confidence Scoring

Each prediction returns a calibrated probability score alongside the binary outcome, providing a clear indication of the model’s confidence in its prediction.
